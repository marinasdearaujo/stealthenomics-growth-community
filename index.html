Content Launch Plan

Posting schedule for the first 6 weeks. 3x/week — Mon, Wed, Fri. Community-first: engagement over content creation. All posts are exclusive and differentiated from the public page — no overlap, no reposts, no recycled content. Text-based questions using Facebook backgrounds — no design needed. Max 2 hours/week on community management. Webinar on the 4th Tuesday of each month. All times EST.

Weekly Formula
Monday = Founders Interaction (recurring series: short talks, interviews, themed discussions with Chris, Paul & Demond) · Wednesday = Polls (simple questions, FB backgrounds, high engagement) · Friday = Friday Wins (community celebration + accountability). Focus is on community engagement, not content creation — this space is a forum where members share, interact and support each other. Simple prompts > polished posts.

Key Rule
Respond to every comment within 2 hours during business hours. Ask a follow-up question. Never let a comment go unanswered in the first 48 hours — this is the single biggest driver of Facebook Group reach.
Future Hire
Dedicated Community Manager recommended when community exceeds 500–750 members. Full-time moderation, advanced analytics, member retention & onboarding optimization.

Growth Roadmap

Four phases from launch to 2,000+ members. Each phase has a clear focus, key actions, and a milestone that signals readiness to move forward.

Phase 1 · Month 1–2
Foundation
Budget: $300 test
  • Seed with 50–100 quality members via personal invites
  • Post 3x/week (Mon · Wed · Fri) — 100% engagement & discussion
  • Respond to every comment within 2 hours
  • Launch Facebook Engagement Ads ($300 test budget)
  • Complete all setup checklist items
Milestone
100 members with 20%+ weekly engagement rate
Phase 2 · Month 2–3
Engagement
Budget: $400–500
  • Optimize Facebook Ads on winning ad
  • Member invitation campaign: "Invite one founder friend"
  • Feature first founder success story
  • First soft CTA: webinar invite or free resource
Milestone
250 members + proven ad formula + first webinar conversions
Phase 3 · Month 4–6
Scale
Budget: $700–800/mo
  • Scale Facebook Ads to $700–800/month
  • Member referral incentive program
  • Cross-platform promotion (LinkedIn, Instagram, Email)
  • Guest expert sessions 1x/month
  • Webinar-to-community funnel
  • Launch community challenges
Milestone
750 members + self-sustaining growth + measurable revenue impact
Phase 4 · Month 7–12
Maturity
Budget: $500–600/mo
  • Reduce ads — focus on retargeting
  • Monthly Talks: Chris / Demond / Paul + guests
  • Video testimonials + founder case studies
  • Community-exclusive offers
  • Community-driven content topics
  • Consider hiring Community Manager
Milestone
2,000 members + community is #1 lead source + positive ROI
